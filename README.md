# Telecom Customer Churn Analysis

## Overview

This project analyzes customer churn for a telecom company using SQL and Power BI. The objective was to identify the major factors contributing to customer churn and recommend actionable business strategies to improve customer retention.

---

## Business Problem

The company has been experiencing a high customer churn rate, leading to revenue loss and increasing customer acquisition costs. Management wants to understand why customers are leaving and identify high-risk customer segments.

---

## Business Objective

- Identify the key drivers of customer churn.
- Analyze customer behavior across contracts, tenure, services, and payment methods.
- Recommend data-driven strategies to improve customer retention.

## Key Findings

- Overall customer churn rate is **26.5%**.
- Customers on **Month-to-Month contracts** have the highest churn.
- Customers within their **first 12 months** are the most likely to leave.
- **Fiber Optic** customers show significantly higher churn than DSL customers.
- Customers without **Tech Support** or **Online Security** churn more frequently.
- Customers using **Electronic Check** have the highest churn among payment methods.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Encourage customers to switch to long-term contracts.
- Improve onboarding during the first year.
- Review pricing and service quality for Fiber Optic plans.
- Bundle Tech Support and Online Security with premium plans.
- Encourage customers to use automatic payment methods.
