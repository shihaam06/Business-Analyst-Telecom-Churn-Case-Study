# Executive Summary

## Project Overview

A telecom company was experiencing a high customer churn rate, resulting in revenue loss and increased customer acquisition costs. This project analyzed customer data using SQL and Power BI to identify the major causes of churn and recommend actionable retention strategies.

---

## Key Insights

- Overall churn rate is 26.5%.
- Month-to-month customers are the highest-risk segment.
- Customers within their first year have the highest churn.
- Fiber Optic customers churn significantly more than DSL customers.
- Customers without Tech Support or Online Security are more likely to leave.
- Electronic Check users have the highest churn among payment methods.

---

## Recommendations

1. Encourage customers to move to long-term contracts.
2. Improve onboarding during the first year.
3. Review Fiber Optic pricing and service quality.
4. Bundle Tech Support and Online Security with premium plans.
5. Encourage customers to use automatic payment methods.

---

## Expected Business Impact

These recommendations can improve customer retention, reduce acquisition costs, increase customer lifetime value, and support sustainable revenue growth.
